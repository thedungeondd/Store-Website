About The Dungeon

Welcome to The Dungeon Smoke Shop, downtown Chico's premier destination for quality smoking products and accessories. Conveniently located at 132 Broadway Street in the heart of downtown Chico, we've established ourselves as a trusted name in the community.

Our shop offers a carefully curated selection of smoking accessories, glassware, and tobacco products. Open daily from 10 AM to midnight, we're here when you need us most. Our knowledgeable staff is dedicated to helping customers find exactly what they're looking for, whether you're a seasoned enthusiast or new to the scene.
